About Our Team

Jennifer Roth

CEO

Jennifer Roth

Jennifer Roth is a senior marketing communications professional with nearly 15 years of experience developing innovative strategies for informing, guiding, and motivating people. Her unique blend of creativity and analytics has created value for a wide variety of organizations, from mid-sized local businesses to Fortune 500 corporations.

Jennifer has a history of turning less-than-ideal circumstances into opportunities. During the oil market downturn of 2015–17, she transformed the media relations program a multinational oilfield services company by integrating content marketing, digital marketing, social media, account-based marketing, and other leading practices. Next, she led the change communications strategy for ExxonMobil transition to cloud computing, applying content strategy alongside traditional change management tactics to guide “old-school” IT professionals up a learning curve that was admittedly steep at times.

As COVID-19 drove the world into an unprecedented disruption, Jennifer decided to lean into the change. She founded Content x Design, a firm whose name reflects her analytical, data-based approach to creative work. Her unique SMART content approach pairs superb creative content with rigorous project management and user research methodologies to deliver specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and timely results.
